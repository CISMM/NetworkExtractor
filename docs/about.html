About Network Extractor

Authors

Network Extractor was written by Cory Quammen at the Center for Computer-Integrated Systems for Microscopy and Manipulation, a National Institute of Biomedical Imaging and Bioengineering resource at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

This work was supported by NIH Grant P41-EB002025-25.
