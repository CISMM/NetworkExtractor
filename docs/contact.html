Contact Us

If you have problems with Fibrin Analysis or want to request a feature, please email developer Cory Quammen (cquammen (at) cs <dot> unc <dot> edu). We cannot guarantee a resolution to your problem or the implementation of your feature request, but we will try to respond to your email and do our best assist you.
